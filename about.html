About Us

Youth Community Development Ghana (YCDG), formerly known as God’s Plan Foundation, is a non-governmental organization established on June 25, 2021. Our goal is to empower youth and work with vulnerable communities to overcome obstacles and challenges that prevent them from living life in its fullness.
