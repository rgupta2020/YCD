About Us

YCDG, formerly known as Godâ€™s Plan Foundation, is a non-governmental organization established on June 25, 2021. Our goal is to empower youth and work with vulnerable communities to overcome obstacles and challenges that prevent them from living life in its fullness.

Our Mission

We bring together and develop a network of Ghanaian youth to share and promote best practices in an interactive, inclusive, and collaborative community. We empower young leaders and provide a voice for rural communities to be heard by Ghanaian institutions and decision-makers.

Our Vision

To be the first point of call in mobilizing funds for eradicating poverty and improving the lives of others. We envision a society where young people in rural communities feel part of a large Ghanaian community where human rights and equality are respected.

Our Projects

YCDG is committed to initiatives like youth participation, intercultural dialogue, mental health awareness, and more. We partner with vulnerable communities and other NGOs to make a lasting impact.
